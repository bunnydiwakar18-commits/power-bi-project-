# ☕ Starbucks Sales Business Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Business Analysis of Starbucks sales data using Power BI.  
The objective is to analyze sales performance, profitability, discount impact, and regional trends to support data-driven decision making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze Total Sales and Profit
- Evaluate Profit Margin performance
- Identify top-performing Products and Regions
- Understand Discount impact on profitability
- Track Monthly Sales Trends

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

- Total Gross Sales
- Total Net Sales
- Total Profit
- Average Profit Margin (%)
- Total Quantity Sold
- Region-wise Profit
- Product-wise Revenue Contribution

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset includes the following fields:

- S.No
- Date
- Month
- Year
- Quarter
- Product
- Region
- Store
- Quantity_Sold
- Unit_Price
- Unit_Cost
- Gross_Sales
- Discount_%
- Discount_Amount
- Net_Sales
- Profit
- Profit_Margin_%

---

## 📈 Dashboard Structure

### 🔹 Page 1 – Executive Overview
- KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Margin, Quantity)
- Monthly Net Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Region-wise Profit (Bar Chart)
- Product Contribution (Donut Chart)
- Interactive Slicers (Year, Region, Product, Store)

### 🔹 Page 2 – Product & Store Analysis
- Product-wise Profitability
- Store Performance Analysis
- Region × Product Matrix

### 🔹 Page 3 – Profit & Discount Analysis
- Discount Impact on Sales
- Profit Margin Trend
- Gross Sales vs Profit (Scatter Analysis)
